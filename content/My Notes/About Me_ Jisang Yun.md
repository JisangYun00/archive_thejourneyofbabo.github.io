## Research & Publications

### Autonomous Driving Architecture Using SLAM and Machine Learning with LiDAR

**Mar 2025 – Dec 2025** | Hanyang University Capstone Design

**Objective:** Development of hybrid autonomous driving system combining traditional SLAM with machine learning for enhanced navigation performance

**Technical Approach:**

- **Perception:** 3D LiDAR SLAM with scan-matcher frontend and graph-based backend
- **Planning:** Hybrid framework integrating global path planning with RL-based local planning (PPO, Imitation Learning)
- **Control:** Pure pursuit controller with optimized trajectory following

**Key Contributions:** Novel integration of classical SLAM techniques with modern ML approaches for robust autonomous navigation

**Technologies:** ROS2, C++, PCL, TensorFlow, 3D LiDAR, F1Tenth platform

_Supervised by Prof. Gichun Cho, Research collaboration with Mingi Ju_
